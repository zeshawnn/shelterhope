Corporate Social Responsibility

There is an increasing number of hospitality service providers. The industry has drastically evolved over the years from franchise hotels and motels to blockchain technology involving independent home owners to gain profit from Airbnbs.
There is a plethora of platforms that can facilitate the increasing statistic of homelessness. Corporate social responsibility is an ever-fading ethos of the white color world. The chances of corporate giving back to their immediate society is deemed even more far-fetched!
Bringing about this realization to these organizations and entities is necessary is the play a large role within society. Our mission aligns itself with the idea of bringing about this realization and providing a solution to maneuver these victims back into civilian life.
